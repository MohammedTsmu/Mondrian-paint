Art Name: Composition No. III, with Red, Blue, Yellow, and Black
Project designed using HTML and CSS only

By: Piet Mondrian
Dutch painter

Painted in 1929, Composition No. III, with Red, Blue, Yellow, and Black is the inaugural canvas in a sequence of paintings–nine in total, all close variations on the same compositional type–that represents the very peak of Mondrian's classicism and the culmination of the entire previous decade's efforts.
